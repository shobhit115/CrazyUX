Why does this exist?

CrazyUX stands for Crazy User Experience - a project built to show that websites do not always need to be serious, perfect, or predictable.

This open-source collection brings together dozens of creative web pages filled with unusual interactions, playful UI ideas, and humorous twists that can make your friends laugh and remind everyone that technology can be fun too.

CrazyUX is a place to explore wild design concepts, learn experimental front-end techniques, and contribute your own crazy page to the community. If you have an idea that breaks the rules of standard UI, this is the perfect home for it.
